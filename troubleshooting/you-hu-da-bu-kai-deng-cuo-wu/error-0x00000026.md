# Error 0x00000026

![Screenshot of the error](../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%285%29.png)

**問題簡述**:‌檔案損毀。‌

**修復方法**:重新下載檔案。‌‌



![How to take the hash of a file](../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%2812%29.png)



安裝 [7-zip](https://www.7-zip.org/) ，右鍵點及檔案，點CRC SHA，選擇SHA-1，你可以比對檔案大小及hash確認是否損毀
